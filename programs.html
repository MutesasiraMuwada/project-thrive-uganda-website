Our Programs

Creating Lasting Change Through Integrated Development

👩‍👧

Gender Equality & Women Empowerment

We promote gender equality by improving access to reproductive health services, supporting girl-child education, amplifying women's voices, and preventing gender-based violence.

  • Reproductive health services
  • Girl-child education support
  • Women's rights advocacy
  • Gender-based violence prevention
📚

Education & Youth Development

We support access to quality education through school support, vocational skills training, facility improvement, and youth entrepreneurship development.

  • School supplies and scholarships
  • Vocational skills training
  • School facility improvement
  • Youth entrepreneurship programs
❤️

Health & Well-Being

Our health programs provide psychosocial support, HIV/AIDS services, mental health awareness, and access to essential medical care for vulnerable populations.

  • Psychosocial support
  • HIV/AIDS services
  • Mental health awareness
  • Essential medical care access
🌾

Food Security & Sustainable Agriculture

We strengthen household food security through nutrition programs, climate-smart agriculture training, farming skills development, and livelihood interventions.

  • Nutrition programs
  • Climate-smart agriculture
  • Farming skills development
  • Livelihood interventions
💧

Water, Sanitation & Hygiene (WASH)

We improve access to safe water and sanitation facilities while promoting hygiene education and environmental protection to support healthier communities.

  • Safe water access
  • Sanitation facilities
  • Hygiene education
  • Environmental protection
🛡️

Child Protection & Social Welfare

We safeguard children from abuse and exploitation by providing legal support, raising community awareness, supporting orphans, and strengthening child protection systems.

  • Legal support for children
  • Community awareness
  • Orphan support programs
  • Child protection systems
💻

Youth Entrepreneurship & ICT

We equip young people with digital skills, business training, financial literacy, and startup development support to enhance employability and innovation.

  • Digital skills training
  • Business training
  • Financial literacy
  • Startup development support
