About Us

Who We Are & What We Stand For

Who We Are

Project Thrive Uganda is a registered non-governmental organisation committed to uplifting vulnerable communities through integrated programs that promote education, health, gender equality, food security, economic empowerment, and child protection.

We believe sustainable development is achieved when communities are empowered to identify their needs and lead solutions that improve their own lives.

Our Vision

A Uganda where all children, youth, and families live dignified, safe, and healthy lives with active participation in their community development.

Our Mission

To empower individuals, families, and communities to live safe, healthy, and sustainable lives.
