About Project Thrive Uganda

Project Thrive Uganda is a community-centered non-profit organisation dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable children, youth, women, and families across Uganda. We implement sustainable programs in health, education, gender equality, WASH, agriculture, youth empowerment, and child protection, working hand-in-hand with communities to create lasting change.

Our Vision

A Uganda where all children, youth, and families live dignified, safe, and healthy lives, with active participation in their community development.

Our Mission

To empower individuals, families, and communities to live safe, healthy, and sustainable lives.

What We Do

👩‍👧

Gender Equality & Women Empowerment

Promoting gender equality through reproductive health services, girl-child education, and preventing gender-based violence.

📚

Education & Youth Development

Supporting access to quality education through school support, vocational skills training, and facility improvement.

❤️

Health & Psychosocial Support

Providing psychosocial support, HIV/AIDS services, mental health awareness, and essential medical care.

🌾

Food Security & Sustainable Agriculture

Strengthening food security through nutrition programs, climate-smart agriculture, and farming skills development.

💧

Water, Sanitation & Hygiene (WASH)

Improving access to safe water and sanitation facilities while promoting hygiene education.

🛡️

Child Protection & Legal Support

Safeguarding children from abuse and exploitation through legal support, awareness, and protection systems.

💻

Youth Entrepreneurship & ICT

Equipping young people with digital skills, business training, financial literacy, and startup development support.
